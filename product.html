Sweetheart (2019)

A young woman named Jenn is stranded on a tropical island after a boating accident. As she struggles to survive, she discovers that the island is not only home to dangerous wildlife, but also a terrifying sea creature that hunts her every night. Jenn must fight for her life and find a way off the island.
